Welcome to Salmon Cookies! Where salmon meets dough to make the moistest cookie known to man

Homegrown. Sustainable. Delicious.

All cookies caught right here on the dock. One by one. Day by day we hire men and women to fish salmon straight out of the puget sound

We use only wooden fishing poles to catch our salmon. We follow this sustainability straight into the kitchen. We have no waste, any garbage gets recycled into food that gets donated to the food Shelter. Anthing that can't be recycled gets turned into awesome street art.
